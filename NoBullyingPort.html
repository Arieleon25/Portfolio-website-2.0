Say No to Bullying

My first major personal project, created in high school in Venezuela in 2011. I developed the story, cast actors, directed, filmed, and edited the short film. It depicts a student experiencing bullying with an impactful ending. The film was showcased in my school and requested by other schools for screenings. It won recognition in my city.
