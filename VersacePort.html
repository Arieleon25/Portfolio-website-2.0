Versace Perfumes Commercial

I worked as a video editor on a Versace commercial produced by Crom Magazine. The commercial featured famous Mexican models, including Luis "Potro" Caballero. The primary focus of my role was to edit the full video, which lasted for 1 minute and was composed of approximately 6 hours of footage from multiple models.

Highlights:

- Achieved over half a million views within the first two weeks of the commercial's release.
- Collaborated with a team of talented professionals to bring the vision of the commercial to life.
- Applied creative editing techniques to enhance the overall aesthetic and appeal of the video.
- Ensured seamless transitions between different shots and models to maintain a cohesive narrative.
- Incorporated visually striking elements to capture the essence of the Versace brand.
- Used my expertise in video editing software to refine the footage and optimize the final product.

Key Responsibilities:

- Reviewing and organizing the raw footage from multiple models.
- Selecting the best shots that aligned with the commercial's objectives and brand identity.
- Trimming and sequencing the footage to create a cohesive storyline.
- Applying color correction and grading techniques to enhance the visual quality.
- Incorporating appropriate music and sound effects to complement the visuals.
- Adding visual effects or motion graphics to elevate the commercial's impact, if required.
- Collaborating closely with the director and other team members to ensure the commercial met the desired vision and goals.
