Treasure Island v.0.2

Gameplay

Treasure Island is an Unreal Engine 5.1 adventure set on an island inspired by Scotland's Dunnottar Castle. Players explore treacherous terrain, collect 250+ coins and gems, dodge AI enemies with full animations, and use power-up gems and checkpoints to survive.

Version 0.2 adds enhanced Win/Game Over/Pause/Settings menus, resolution options from Low to Ultra, nanite foliage, and dynamic gem respawn.

Tools & Workflow

Built with Unreal Engine 5.1 using a mix of C++ and Blueprints, coded in VS Code, and version-controlled through GitHub.
