Who We Are

About Us

Welcome to VinciVision LLC, where creativity meets data-driven results. We specialize in crafting high-performing video ads that help businesses of all sizes grow their sales, reach, and audience engagement. Whether you're a startup, an established brand, or an agency in need of freelance support, our team is here to bring your vision to life.

Our Expertise

With years of experience in video editing, creative production, and ad performance analysis, VinciVision LLC is uniquely positioned to deliver exceptional results. Our founder, Arie, has worked with major clients such as TubeScience, Lumin, Fabletics, and Cerebral, editing thousands of video ads across platforms like Meta, TikTok, YouTube, and Amazon. We excel in turning raw footage into data-driven video creatives that drive measurable performance improvements.

What Sets Us Apart

  • Data-Driven Creativity: We combine analytics and creative expertise to create video ads that don't just look great but deliver results.
  • Flexible Solutions: Acting as an external partner, we adapt to your needs without the overhead or ad commission fees of traditional agencies.
  • End-to-End Services: From strategy development to creative production, ad performance tracking, and consulting, we offer comprehensive support for your paid social video campaigns.
